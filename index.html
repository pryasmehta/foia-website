Who We Are

A bridge between Czech & Indian cultures

Friends of India Association (FOIA) is a civic association registered in the Czech Republic, uniting people who share a deep appreciation for India — its culture, heritage, values, and people.

Founded on the principles of friendship, cultural exchange, and mutual understanding, FOIA serves as a platform for Czech and Indian communities to connect, collaborate, and celebrate together.

What We Do

Main Activities
of the Association

Our association is active across four key areas that bring our mission to life.

01
🎭

Cultural Events & Celebrations

Organizing festivals, exhibitions, and cultural evenings that showcase the rich diversity of Indian traditions — from classical arts to contemporary expressions — for the Czech public.

02
🤝

Community Integration & Networking

Facilitating connections between Indian nationals living in the Czech Republic and the local Czech community, supporting integration and mutual understanding through regular meet-ups and networking events.

03
📚

Education & Cultural Awareness

Running workshops, seminars, and educational programs about Indian history, philosophy, cuisine, language, and arts — fostering genuine curiosity and appreciation among Czech citizens.

04
🌏

Bilateral Cooperation & Exchange

Supporting Czech–Indian bilateral relations through cooperation with embassies, institutions, businesses, and NGOs, and facilitating exchange programs that deepen ties between both nations.

Our Purpose

The Goal of
the Association

The primary goal of FOIA is to nurture a genuine, lasting friendship between the Czech and Indian peoples — built not on formality, but on shared experiences, open dialogue, and heartfelt connection.

We strive to make India accessible to Czech society and to make Czech culture welcoming to Indians — creating a two-way bridge of understanding that enriches both communities and strengthens the bonds between our two nations.

"To cultivate friendship, foster cultural understanding, and create a lasting bond between the people of Czech Republic and India."
Why It Matters

Added Value of
the Association

A Welcoming Home for Indians

For Indian nationals in the Czech Republic, FOIA provides a sense of community, belonging, and support — a familiar space in a foreign land where culture and connection are celebrated.

Cultural Enrichment for Czech Society

FOIA brings the depth and color of Indian civilization to Czech audiences — broadening perspectives, inspiring curiosity, and enriching the cultural fabric of Czech society.

Strengthening Bilateral Ties

By connecting people at a grassroots level, FOIA contributes to the broader diplomatic and economic relationship between the Czech Republic and India — people-to-people diplomacy in action.
